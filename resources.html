Government Schemes

National Mental Health Programme (NMHP)

Launched by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, NMHP aims to improve the accessibility and affordability of mental healthcare services across India.

Website Link

District Mental Health Programme (DMHP)

A component of NMHP, DMHP focuses on providing mental healthcare services at the district level.

Website Link

Mental Health Helpline 181

This toll-free helpline provides information and support on mental health issues.

Website Link

National Health Mission (NHM)

NHM includes a mental health component that provides funding for mental healthcare services in states.
